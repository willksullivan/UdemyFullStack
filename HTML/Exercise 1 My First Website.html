The Witcher 3

The Witcher 3 is based in a non technology driven world with moderate amounts of magic. In this world, swords, shields, bows, and magic are the weapons of the era. You take the role of Geralt of Rivia, a man who has undergone mutation in order to possess super human speed and strength.
